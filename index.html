About Me

I am an entrepreneur and trained chemist who has faith in the power of storytelling, especially on the web, can bring us together to make real change in this world.

While I am sometimes mistaken for an internet villain with a similar sounding name, I'm best known as the founder of Tait Laboratories Inc., a company based in Canada making healthy new ingredients for food, beverages and natural health products using citrus peel waste left over from the juice industry.

Combining my degrees in science with a drive to help others creatively design and tell their stories across the web, I offer consulting services to assist others who work to catalyze sustained global change. Click the button below to see my CV at github.
